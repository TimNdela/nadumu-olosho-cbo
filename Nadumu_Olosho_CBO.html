About Nadumu Olosho

Our Story

Founded by Victor Solitei, Nadumu Olosho began as a small initiative to address the pressing needs of marginalized communities in our region. What started as a passion project has now grown into a full-fledged community-based organization making tangible impacts.

We believe in the power of collective action and community-driven solutions to create sustainable change.

Victor Solitei

Founder & Director

Our Mission

To empower youth, marginalized communities, and the needy through education, skills development, and sustainable livelihood programs that foster self-reliance and community resilience.

Our Vision

A society where every individual, regardless of their background, has equal opportunities to thrive and contribute meaningfully to community development.

Youth Education Forum – Success Story

On August 23rd, 2024, Nadumu Olosho in partnership with KCA University, Umma University, East African University, Kajiado County Youth Alliance, and other important stakeholder in the county's education held a highly successful Youth Education Forum at PEFA Church, Imaroro Town. The event empowered youth with skills, guidance, and inspiration through engaging workshops, panel discussions, and career mentorship. Summary of the event:

  • Motivational talks by education leaders
  • Career guidance and networking
  • Tree planting and sanitary pads donation
  • Interactive Workshops and Q&A Sessions

Attendees included high school and university/college students, recent graduates, parents, and local leaders. The forum fostered hope and direction for many youths in our community.
